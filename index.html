Visibility & Insights

Know what browsers and add-ons exist in your environment before enforcing policy.

  • Browser inventory & version insights Get visibility into browsers in your network and their versions to identify outdated/risky instances.
  • Add-on discovery Track extensions/plugins across endpoints and spot high-risk or unapproved add-ons.
  • Usage reporting Analyze browsing trends and policy impact through centralized reports.

Security Hardening & Addon Control

Harden browser settings and control extensions to reduce browser-based attack vectors.

  • Secure browser configurations Apply configurations via policy to protect browsers against common security breaches.
  • Extension / plugin management Allowlist/blocklist add-ons to minimize exposure from malicious extensions.
  • Browser restriction Enforce approved browsers across your environment to keep browsing standardized.

Web Access Controls

Restrict unsafe browsing behavior while keeping users productive.

  • Web filtering Control access to websites/domains/categories based on business needs.
  • Download restriction Prevent risky downloads from untrusted sources to reduce malware entry points.
  • Browser isolation Isolate risky web sessions away from enterprise data to reduce web-borne threats.
